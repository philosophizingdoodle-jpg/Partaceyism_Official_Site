This is Respectivism. Respectivism is the view that reality is grounded in an infinite coherent will whose address makes obligation binding, while Respect is the structure in which beings participate that makes coherence intelligible but not authoritative. Respectivism is an metaphysical and existential philosophy, combining Nietzchean Philosophy and Christian Process Theology. I developed Respectivism as a way to respect my own conditions on my busy life of two jobs. Please enjoy the wonderful work of An Inquiry On Respect.
