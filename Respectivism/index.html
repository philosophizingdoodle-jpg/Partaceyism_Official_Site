The Works Of Partaceyism

These three books are the foundational texts of the economic, philosophical, and societal view of Partaceyism. Partaceyism is the view that economics and society should be aligned with mutual respect through the establishment of an emotional market. Partaceyism, or part-a-governmentism, makes free market logic subservient to mutual recognition. I developed Partaceyism to solve our climate crisis, our social crisis, and our crisis with inflation. I want to live in hope, not in spite of it.

Within An Inquiry On Respect, a fusion philosophy of Nietzschean Overcoming and Christian Process Theology, or Respectivism, is developed and explained in detail. Jay's Struggle is a novel that shows humanity how we can practically progress towards Partaceyism, while exploring the existential struggles of Jay Tabor. Please enjoy the wonderful works of The Partaceyist Statement, Jay's Struggle, and An Inquiry On Respect.
