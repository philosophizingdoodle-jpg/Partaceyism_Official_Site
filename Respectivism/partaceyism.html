The Works Of Partaceyism

These three books are the foundational texts of the economic, philosophical, and societial view of Partaceyism. Partaceyism is the view that economics and society should be aligned with mutual respect, via the establishment of an emotional market. Partaceyism, or part-a-governmentism, makes free market logic subservient to mutual recognition. I developed Partaceyism to solve our climate crisis, our social crisis, and our crissi with inflation. I want to live in hope, not to live in spite of it.

Within An Inquiry On Respect, an fusion philosophy of Nietzchean Overcoming and Christian Process Theology, or Respectvism, is developed and expalined in detail. Jay's Struggle is an novel that shows humanity how we can pratically progress towards Partaceyism, while exploring the existential struggles of Jay Tabor. Please enjoy the wonderful works of The Partaceyist Statement, Jay's Struggle, and An Inquiry On Respect.
